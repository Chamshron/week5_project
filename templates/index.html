What is the European Kendo League?

The European Kendo Federation (EKF) is the overall non profit organization for the traditional Japanese martial arts of Kendo, Jodo and Iaido with more than 40 Member Federations in Europe. We organize the European Kendo League to allow individuals to create teams and compete at a high level outside of representing their country at an international level.
